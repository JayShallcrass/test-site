Looking to buy, sell, rent or let in Peterborough?


Then look no further!

We offer a wide range of stress-free services and solutions, including: lettings, residential sales, and landlord insurance to suit all your property needs. So whether you're buying, letting, renting, or selling, Northwood is here for you every step of the way!

As a leading Peterborough estate agent, we're a professional team of local, friendly experts who are passionate about property. We offer the full range of sales, lettings and property management services, but are best known for our Guaranteed rent service - the perfect let it and forget it solution for landlords.

Northwood Peterborough was founded in 2006 and is well known for lettings and estate agency across Peterborough, Cambridgeshire & Lincolnshire.

Since opening over 12 years ago our key goal has been to deliver outstanding customer service, due to this approach we are proud customer service, Gold award winners for The Best Letting Agent in the UK for the last 3 years. These All Agent awards are voted for by customers who rate the service of their agent, we call it the Trip Advisor for the Property Industry.

 

Buying - We believe that in order to achieve the right property for you it's vital to understand your particular needs to make the process as successful and stress-free as possible. Our team of friendly experts will work with you to achieve your goal.

Selling - We promise that there are no hard sales techniques here, just straight-talking professionals who genuinely enjoy getting the best sale for their customers. So get in touch even if you are just considering selling and want some advice, would like a no obligation valuation or want to find out what the perfect sales package could look like for you.

We'd love the opportunity to provide you with a free valuation, so if you're thinking of letting or selling a property in Peterborough, Cambridgeshire or Lincolnshire, give us a call today on 01733 890082

As a responsible and ethical agent we hold clients money protection insurance through NALS
